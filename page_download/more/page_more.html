Flux DNS is a powerful and lightweight DNS changer designed to improve your internet experience on Android devices. Whether you’re looking for faster browsing, better security, or access to restricted content, Flux DNS delivers a seamless solution — without needing root access.

Key Features

  • One-Tap DNS Change – Instantly switch to trusted DNS servers like Google, Cloudflare, or custom ones.
  • Privacy Protection – Keep your data safe with encrypted DNS support (DNS over HTTPS/DoH).
  • Speed Boost – Reduce latency and improve connection speed by using faster DNS providers.
  • No Root Required – Works smoothly on all Android 9+ devices without root access.
  • Clean & Minimal UI – Designed for simplicity and performance.

Perfect for gamers, streamers, or anyone who wants more control over their network, Flux DNS gives you the power to browse freely and securely — all in one sleek app.
